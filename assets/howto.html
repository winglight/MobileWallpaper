How to set a live wallpaper

Android 2.1 allows you to set live wallpapers. These are wallpapers that update with information or images without user intervention – they update automatically at regular intervals. Live wallpapers are a great way to stay on top of news or updates as they happen. Here’s how you can set live wallpapers on Android.

STEP 1. Tap and hold

Press the home button to first go to the homescreen. On the homescreen, just tap and hold on any blank spot until a context menu appears or press the menu button.

STEP 2. Tap on “Wallpaper” and then “Live Wallpaper”

In the menu or dialog box that showed up in STEP 1, just tap on the “Wallpaper” option, and then the “Live Wallpaper” menu item.

STEP 3. Select your choice of Live Wallpaper : Daily Mobile Wallpaper

You’ll be presented with a selection of available Live Wallpapers. You’ll see it going through the same motions that you can expect to see as a Live Wallpaper on your Android phone. Use the back button to go back to the list of all Live Wallpapers. You can only set one Live Wallpaper at a time.

STEP 4. Tap “Set Wallpaper”

Once you’ve decided on the Live Wallpaper you want, namely Daily Mobile Wallpaper, simply tap “Set Wallpaper” and the Live Wallpaper will be installed and deployed immediately on your Android smartphone.
